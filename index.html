About "Me"

I grew up in Waterford, MI and lived in that part of the country until I was 23. In 2018, I moved to Durango, CO to pursue a short-lived career as a professional mountain biker. Then, in 2021, I moved to New York city, where I currently reside.

With much excitement and anticipation, I started my Thinkful journey in February of 2022. I have always been a bit resistant to the idea of pursuing this particular career as both my dad and my grandfather are computer programmers and I felt compelled to forge my own path, despite all the signs telling me this would be a good idea.

It feels great to have finally accepted the fact that this was probably my destiny all along. I am eagerly looking forward to diving head-first into the world of web development!
